Gutting the van

After removing the carpet, I found this sound deadening material on the van floor which had already started to peel off. So I got to work and chiseled it all off.

Painting

Painted the floor to prevent future rust problems.

Floor Insulation

I cut out 1 inch polysio foam board to fit around the floor ridges. Afterwards, I cut out 1/2 inch foam board pieces to fit inside the 1 inch cutout over the ridges. This was probably overkill, but the project was fresh and I was striving for perfection.

Subfloor

Cut out 1/4 inch pine plywood to shape.

Ceiling insulation

Stuck 1 inch polysio foam board to the ceiling and filled gaps with 'Great Stuff Gap Filler'.
